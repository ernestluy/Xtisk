购票须知

一、购票方式
1.所有航线售票截止时间为开航时间前1天，只可购买15天以内的船票。
2.每次交易，只能订同一航线同一航班船票，且最多可以购买10张。订购不同航线不同航班的船票请分开重新购票。
3.只能购买成人、儿童两种类型船票。香港航线及澳门航线：一岁至六岁(不含六岁)的儿童可购买小童票，六岁(含六岁)以上需购买全票；珠海航线：1.2米以下的儿童需到现场领取免费儿童票，1.2-1.5米儿童可购买小童票，1.5米以上需购买全票。其他票类型的船票需到蛇口客运码头售票处购买。
4. 必须提供取票人姓名、取票证件号码最后三位、手机号码、E-mail等信息，且必须正确填写，仔细核对后提交。交易一经产生就不能取消，费用即从帐户扣除，用户通过手机购票成功后，用户通过手机购票成功后，系统会自动发送交易订单号到用户所留手机号及邮箱上。
二、取票地点及时间
1．用户持交易订单号及必要证件到蛇口客运码头自取。
2.购蛇口至香港中环，澳门航线船票的旅客，取票人必须于所乘航班开航前60分钟到蛇口客运码头港澳售票处取票。
3.订购珠海航线船票的旅客：取票人必须于所乘航班开航前60分钟到蛇口客运码头国内售票处取票。
三、退票方式
如购票后需办理退票的，取票人必须在所购船票开航时间前60分钟，携交易订单号及取票证件亲临蛇口客运码头港澳或国内相应售票处办理退票手续并收取相应退票手续费，开航后本公司概不安排办理退款、退票。

Instructions for Online Ticket Service
Passenger must obey the Ticket Purchase Instructions of the Shekou Ferry Terminal as well as all other relevant regulations, and must be responsible for all content related to the online ticket service.
A、Tickets Purchase Methods:
1. We will stop selling the tickets for all sailing routes 1 day before the departure, and tickets can only be bought for 15 days in advance.
2.Customer can only book up 10 pieces of ferry tickets of same shipping line and same ferry in each transaction .if customer wants to book another shipping or ferry, Please restart a new transaction.
3.Only two types of tickets are available online: adults and children. For sailing routes heading for Hong Kong and Macao: children tickets can be bought for those below six years old, and full price tickets are necessary for those above six; for sailing routes heading for Zhuhai: children tickets can be bought for those within 1.2-1.5 meters high, and full price tickets are necessary for those above 1.5 meters. Other types of tickets must be bought at the ticket office of the Shekou Ferry Terminal.
4. Information such as the name, ID No, mobile number, E-mail, etc, of the person who collects the ticket must be provided, correctly filled up, and handed in after confirmation. Once the trade is made, it cannot be cancelled, and the fees will be taken from your account. After the ticket is successfully purchased from the application,The trade transaction number will send to customer’s mobile phone and E-mail box from the system automatically..
B、Tickets collects and time
1. Passengers need to collects the tickets by bringing the trade transaction number and the necessary certificate to the Shekou Ferry Terminal.
2 .For passengers who have booked tickets heading for Central Hong Kong or Macao: the person to collect the ticket must arrive at the Hong Kong/Macao ticket office and collect the ticket 60 minutes before the departure.
3. For passengers who have booked tickets heading for Zhuhai: the person to collect the ticket must arrive at the domestic ticket office and collect the ticket 60 minutes before the departure.
C、Tickets cancelled methods:
If the purchased ticket needs to be cancelled, the person to collect the ticket must, 60 minutes before the departure, bring the printed information for the successful trade and the certificate necessary for collecting the ticket to the Shekou Ferry Terminal him/herself and go through the ticket cancellation procedures at the Hong Kong and Macao ticket office or the domestic ticket office, and certain amount of cancellation fee is chargeable. If the ticket expires, Shekou Ferry Terminal  will not accept any cancellation or demand for reimbursement.
